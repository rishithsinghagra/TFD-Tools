The First Descendant HP / Defense Calculator

This tool helps you compare effective HP with different HP / Defense combinations.

The question mark button next to each field is unlocked when the other 3 fields are filled. It calculates the value required in that field to make both combinations have equal effective HP.

Given the information the community has, I am unsure of how damage reduction for defense values of less than 1000 are calculated, and as such, they are set to 0% damage reduction here.
